Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredient Section

  • 2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cup sweet choco chips
